# Fret Not v1.4.1 - App Store Release Notes

## What's New in Version 1.4.1

We've given Fret Not a beautiful visual refresh and optimized the entire experience for iPad!

### Optimized for iPad
The app now takes full advantage of your iPad's larger screen with thoughtfully constrained layouts and adaptive spacing. All pages use a comfortable reading width, and modals present full-screen for an immersive editing experience. Every interaction feels perfectly suited to your device.

### A Fresh New Look
The entire app now features a warm, cohesive design with subtle cream backgrounds and elegant card styling. Every screen has been carefully refined for a more polished, professional appearance.

### Easier Chord Management
• **Quick chord suggestions** - When creating custom chords, tap any matched chord name to instantly add it to your song
• **Custom voicings made simple** - Tap any chord diagram and choose "Create Custom Variation" to add your own fingerings
• **Better Spotify integration** - Improved search results with clearer card styling

### Polish Throughout
Every text field, button, and card has been refined with smooth animations and consistent styling. The app feels more responsive and delightful to use on both iPhone and iPad.
