# Fret Not v1.4.1 - App Store Release Notes

## What's New in Version 1.4.1

We've given Fret Not a beautiful visual refresh and made it easier than ever to manage your chord library!

### A Fresh New Look
The entire app now features a warm, cohesive design with subtle cream backgrounds and elegant card styling. Every screen has been carefully refined for a more polished, professional appearance.

### Easier Chord Management
• **Quick chord suggestions** - When creating custom chords, tap any matched chord name to instantly add it to your song
• **Custom voicings made simple** - Tap any chord diagram and choose "Create Custom Variation" to add your own fingerings
• **Better Spotify integration** - Improved search results with clearer card styling

### Polish Throughout
Every text field, button, and card has been refined with smooth animations and consistent styling. The app feels more responsive and delightful to use.
